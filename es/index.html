Política de privacidad

Introducción: Fuelaris Capital processes personal data to provide institutional investment communications.

Recopilación de datos: We collect name, professional email, phone number, and interaction metadata.

Base legal: Processing is based on legitimate interest, pre-contractual requests, and compliance obligations.

Cookies: Essential and analytics cookies are used for service quality and aggregated behavior measurement.

Conservación: Data is retained only as long as necessary for regulatory, contractual, and operational requirements.

Compartición con terceros: We share data with regulated service providers under confidentiality and processing agreements.

Seguridad: Encryption, access controls, monitoring, and incident response frameworks protect your information.

Derechos del usuario: You may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, and portability where legally applicable.

Transferencias internacionales: Cross-border transfers rely on approved safeguards and contractual protections.

Divulgación de riesgo del sector: Investment communications may include forward-looking assumptions and risk scenarios.
